研究倫理與資料治理

IRB、HIPAA、GDPR 與 FAIR 原則：在資料庫變大、AI 普及的時代，如何負責任地做研究。

IRB, HIPAA, GDPR, and FAIR: how to do responsible research in an age of big data and AI.

概覽

為什麼倫理決定能否發表？

當代生醫研究的兩大規範來源：(1) 研究倫理——保護受試者；(2) 資料治理——管理資料如何被存取、共享、再利用。違反任一者，輕則 paper 被撤稿、重則機構喪失 NIH 資助資格。AI/ML 時代又新增了演算法公平性、可解釋性等議題。

Modern biomedical research lives by two governance pillars: (1) research ethics — protecting subjects; (2) data governance — managing how data is accessed, shared, and reused. Violating either can mean retraction or, worse, loss of NIH funding eligibility. The AI/ML era adds algorithmic fairness and explainability concerns.

🚨
歷史教訓：Tuskegee 梅毒實驗 (1932-1972) 與 Henrietta Lacks 細胞案 (1951) 直接催生了《貝爾蒙報告》(1979) 與當代知情同意制度。 Lessons from history: The Tuskegee syphilis study (1932-1972) and the Henrietta Lacks case (1951) directly led to the Belmont Report (1979) and modern informed consent.
貝爾蒙三原則

一、貝爾蒙三大原則

🙏

Respect for Persons

尊重人格：受試者有自主決定權，知情同意 (Informed Consent) 須包含目的、流程、風險、退出權利。對自主性受限者（兒童、認知障礙者）需額外保護。

Subjects have autonomy. Informed consent must cover purpose, procedures, risks, and right to withdraw. Vulnerable populations (children, cognitively impaired) need extra protection.

💚

Beneficence

有利原則：研究的預期效益要大於風險。需做風險-效益評估，並使用最小風險的設計達成目標。

Expected benefits must outweigh risks. A formal risk-benefit assessment is required, and minimal-risk designs are preferred.

⚖️

Justice

正義原則：研究負擔與受益的公平分配。不能讓弱勢族群只承擔風險、未受益（避免重蹈 Tuskegee）。

Equitable distribution of research burdens and benefits. Vulnerable groups should not bear the risks while others reap the rewards (the Tuskegee lesson).

FAIR 原則

三、FAIR 資料原則

2016 年由 Force11 提出，已成為 NIH/EU 資料管理計畫的標準：

Coined by Force11 in 2016, now the standard for NIH/EU data management plans:

🔍 Findable

資料有持久識別碼 (DOI、ORCID、accession number)、豐富 metadata、能被索引引擎找到。

Data has persistent identifiers (DOI, ORCID, accession), rich metadata, and is indexed by search engines.

🔓 Accessible

透過標準協定取得（HTTPS、API），即便資料受控、metadata 仍應公開。

Retrievable via standardized protocols (HTTPS, APIs). Even for controlled-access data, metadata should be public.

🔗 Interoperable

採用共通詞彙 (Ontology, MeSH, HPO, SNOMED)、標準格式 (VCF, BAM, BIDS)。

Use shared vocabularies (ontologies, MeSH, HPO, SNOMED) and standard formats (VCF, BAM, BIDS).

♻️ Reusable

明確的授權條款（CC-BY、CC0、GPL）、來源紀錄 (provenance)、社群標準。

Clear license (CC-BY, CC0, GPL), provenance metadata, conformance to community standards.

AI 倫理

四、生醫 AI 倫理新議題

📊 演算法公平性

2019 Obermeyer 在 Science 揭露：美國醫療資源分配演算法因以「醫療支出」當作健康指標，對黑人病患低估健康風險。
對策：subgroup analysis、equalized odds、demographic parity 檢驗。

Obermeyer (Science, 2019) showed a US care-allocation algorithm systematically underrated risk in Black patients because it used cost as a health proxy.
Mitigations: subgroup analysis, equalized odds, demographic parity audits.

🔁 模型再識別風險

基因組資料即便去名也可能透過 SNP 組合反查到本人 (Gymrek 2013)；GAN 生成的醫學影像可能洩漏訓練樣本資訊。對策：differential privacy、k-anonymity、federated learning。

De-identified genomes can be linked back via SNP combinations (Gymrek 2013); GAN-generated medical images may leak training samples. Mitigations: differential privacy, k-anonymity, federated learning.

📋 知情同意的更新

傳統一次性同意難涵蓋「未來的 AI 再分析」。新型 dynamic / broad consent 讓參與者可動態調整授權，但需配套追蹤機制。

One-time consent cannot anticipate future AI reanalysis. Dynamic/broad consent lets participants update permissions but requires robust tracking.

🌍 代表性偏倚

GWAS 樣本 ~80% 為歐裔，導致 polygenic risk score 在非歐裔表現大幅下降。對策：H3Africa、PAGE、All of Us 等多元 cohort。

~80% of GWAS samples are European; PRS performance drops sharply in non-European populations. Initiatives like H3Africa, PAGE, and All of Us tackle this.

實作流程

IRB 申請流程概要

判斷研究類別

Exempt（豁免）/ Expedited（加速）/ Full Review（完整審查）。次級分析去識別資料常屬 Exempt Category 4。

Exempt / Expedited / Full Review. Secondary analysis of de-identified data is often Exempt Category 4.

準備文件

研究計畫書、知情同意書、招募廣告、CV、利益衝突聲明、CITI 證書。

Protocol, informed consent form, recruitment ads, CVs, COI disclosures, CITI training certificate.

提交與審查

經 IRB 預審 → 委員會審查（如需）→ 修正回覆 → 核准函。完整審查 4-8 週。

Pre-review → committee review (if needed) → revisions → approval letter. Full review takes 4-8 weeks.

持續義務

SAE 通報、年度回顧、計畫修訂、結案報告。

SAE reporting, annual review, amendments, final report.

📝 隨堂測驗

Q1. HIPAA Safe Harbor 要移除幾項？

A. 10
B. 12
C. 18
D. 25
HIPAA Safe Harbor 列出 18 項必須移除的識別資訊，從姓名到 IP 都包含。HIPAA Safe Harbor lists 18 identifiers — from names to IP addresses.

Q2. 貝爾蒙報告不含哪一項？

A. Respect for Persons
B. Beneficence
C. Justice
D. Profit
三大原則為尊重人格、有利、正義。「利潤」並非倫理原則。The three principles are Respect for Persons, Beneficence, Justice. "Profit" is not one of them.

Q3. FAIR 中的「I」代表？

A. Independent
B. Identifiable
C. Interoperable
D. Indexable
FAIR = Findable / Accessible / Interoperable / Reusable。FAIR = Findable / Accessible / Interoperable / Reusable.

Q4. 處理歐盟居民健康資料的規範？

A. HIPAA
B. GDPR
C. FAIR
D. CITI
GDPR 為歐盟通用資料保護法規，對歐洲居民的個資處理有嚴格要求。GDPR is the EU's General Data Protection Regulation, with strict rules for processing EU residents' data.
