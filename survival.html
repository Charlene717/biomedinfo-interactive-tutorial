存活分析

時間到事件（time-to-event）資料的核心方法：Kaplan-Meier、log-rank 與 Cox 比例風險模型。

Core methods for time-to-event data: Kaplan-Meier, log-rank, and Cox proportional hazards.

概覽

存活資料的特殊性

存活分析處理的資料變數有兩個元件：(1) 時間（從某起點到事件發生）；(2) 事件指標（是否觀察到事件）。它不能用普通迴歸處理，因為：

  • Censoring（設限）：研究結束時許多人「還沒事件發生」——這仍是有資訊的觀察
  • 時間非常態：存活時間分布常呈右偏
  • 追蹤時間不等長

「事件」可以是死亡、復發、感染、戒菸成功等。雖然方法源自工程的「故障時間」分析，但生醫場域是最大應用領域。

Survival data has two parts: (1) time (from start to event); (2) event indicator. It can't use ordinary regression because:

  • Censoring: many subjects haven't had the event yet at study end — still informative
  • Non-normal: survival times are right-skewed
  • Unequal follow-up

"Events" may be death, recurrence, infection, smoking cessation — biomedicine is the largest user despite engineering origins.

💡
Censoring 不是缺失值！它是「至少存活到某時間」的資訊，存活分析方法能正確利用。直接刪除 censored 資料會產生嚴重偏差。 Censoring is not missing data! It says "survived at least until t" — survival methods use this correctly. Dropping censored rows badly biases results.
核心概念

一、存活函數與風險函數

S(t) — Survival

存活至時間 t 仍未發生事件的機率：S(t) = P(T > t)。S(0)=1，並單調下降至 0。Kaplan-Meier 是估計 S(t) 的標準方法。

Probability of surviving past time t: S(t) = P(T > t). S(0)=1, monotonically decreasing to 0. Kaplan-Meier estimates S(t).

h(t) — Hazard

瞬時風險：給定存活至 t，在下一刻發生事件的條件機率率。Cox 模型直接對 hazard 建模。

Instantaneous risk: conditional on surviving to t, rate of event in the next instant. Cox models h(t) directly.

Censoring

研究結束、退出或失訪時尚未發生事件 → right-censored。需假設 censoring 機制獨立於風險（即 informative censoring 會破壞估計）。

No event by study end / dropout / loss-to-follow-up → right-censored. Methods assume censoring is independent of risk (informative censoring breaks estimates).

Kaplan-Meier

二、Kaplan-Meier 與 log-rank

Kaplan-Meier (KM) 在每個觀察到的事件時間更新存活機率：
S(ti) = S(ti-1) × (1 - di/ni)，其中 di 是 ti 的死亡數、ni 是當時 at risk 的人數。
KM 曲線呈現「階梯下降」，每次階梯處有事件發生，小垂線「|」表示 censoring。

Log-rank 檢定比較兩條或多條 KM 曲線：在每個事件時間比較「觀察到的死亡數」與「在 H₀（兩組風險相同）下期望的死亡數」，加總後得到 chi-square 統計量。

Kaplan-Meier (KM) updates survival at each observed event:
S(ti) = S(ti-1) × (1 − di/ni), where di = deaths at ti, ni = at-risk count.
The KM curve is a step function — each step is an event; vertical ticks mark censoring.

Log-rank test compares two or more KM curves: at each event time, compare observed vs expected deaths under H₀ (equal hazards), then sum into a chi-square.

⚠️
log-rank 的限制：若兩條曲線會交叉（hazard 比例違反），log-rank 檢定力大降。改用 Renyi、Fleming-Harrington、或 Restricted Mean Survival Time (RMST) 比較。 Log-rank caveat: If curves cross (proportional hazards violated), log-rank loses power. Use Renyi, Fleming-Harrington, or RMST comparisons instead.

Cox 模型

三、Cox 模型

當需要同時控制多個共變數（如年齡、性別、分期），改用 Cox 模型：

h(t | X) = h0(t) · exp(β1X1 + β2X2 + … + βkXk)

其中 h0(t) 為基線風險（不需指定形狀，semi-parametric）；exp(β) 即為對應變數每增加 1 單位的hazard ratio (HR)。

核心假設：比例風險 (PH assumption)——兩組或不同共變數值的風險比隨時間維持定值。檢驗方式：

  • Schoenfeld residuals 檢定 (cox.zph)
  • log-log plot：log(-log S(t)) vs log(t) 應平行
  • 時間互動項顯著表示違反 PH

違反 PH 時的對策：分層 Cox、時間相依共變數、加速失敗時間 (AFT) 模型、Restricted Mean Survival Time。

To adjust for multiple covariates (age, sex, stage), use the Cox model:

h(t | X) = h0(t) · exp(β1X1 + β2X2 + … + βkXk)

where h0(t) is the baseline hazard (unspecified shape — semi-parametric); exp(β) is the hazard ratio (HR) per unit increase in X.

Key assumption — Proportional Hazards: the hazard ratio is constant over time. Check via:

  • Schoenfeld residuals test (cox.zph)
  • log-log plot: log(-log S(t)) vs log(t) should be parallel
  • Significant time-interaction terms violate PH

If PH is violated: stratified Cox, time-varying covariates, AFT models, RMST.

程式碼

實作範例

library(survival); library(survminer)
data(lung)   # NCCTG lung cancer

# 1. Kaplan-Meier
fit <- survfit(Surv(time, status) ~ sex, data=lung)
ggsurvplot(fit, pval=TRUE, conf.int=TRUE, risk.table=TRUE)

# 2. Log-rank test
survdiff(Surv(time, status) ~ sex, data=lung)

# 3. Cox PH
cox <- coxph(Surv(time, status) ~ age + sex + ph.ecog, data=lung)
summary(cox)   # exp(coef) = HR with 95% CI and p

# 4. PH assumption check
zph <- cox.zph(cox)
print(zph); plot(zph)
from lifelines import KaplanMeierFitter, CoxPHFitter
from lifelines.statistics import logrank_test
import pandas as pd

df = pd.read_csv("lung.csv")   # columns: time, status, age, sex, ph.ecog

# 1. KM
kmf = KaplanMeierFitter()
for name, grp in df.groupby("sex"):
    kmf.fit(grp["time"], grp["status"], label=f"sex={name}")
    kmf.plot_survival_function()

# 2. Log-rank
m = df["sex"]==1
r = logrank_test(df.time[m], df.time[~m],
                 event_observed_A=df.status[m],
                 event_observed_B=df.status[~m])
print(r.p_value)

# 3. Cox PH
cph = CoxPHFitter()
cph.fit(df, duration_col="time", event_col="status")
cph.print_summary()

# 4. PH check
cph.check_assumptions(df, p_value_threshold=0.05)

📝 隨堂測驗

Q1. Censored 觀察應如何處理？

A. 直接刪除Drop them
B. 補成最大時間Impute with max time
C. 用存活分析方法（KM/Cox）正確利用Properly handled by survival methods (KM/Cox)
D. 用平均存活時間取代Replace with mean survival
Censoring 是「至少存活到 t」的部分資訊，KM 與 Cox 透過 at-risk 集合正確使用。直接刪除會嚴重低估存活機率。Censoring carries the information "survived at least to t." KM and Cox handle it via the at-risk set. Dropping severely underestimates survival.

Q2. Cox 模型的核心假設？

A. 事件時間呈常態Normality of event times
B. 比例風險（HR 隨時間定值）Proportional hazards (HR constant over time)
C. 基線風險為指數分配Baseline hazard is exponential
D. 大樣本Large sample size
Cox 模型不對基線 hazard 形狀做假設（semi-parametric），但要求各組 HR 隨時間維持定值，即「比例風險」假設。Cox is semi-parametric — no assumption on baseline hazard shape — but requires constant HR over time: the PH assumption.

Q3. HR = 0.5 代表？

A. 存活時間多一半Survival time doubles
B. 任一時刻的瞬時風險為對照組的一半Instantaneous risk is half that of control at any time
C. 50% 病人會存活50% of patients survive
D. p 值為 0.5p-value is 0.5
HR 是 hazard 的比例，反映「相對」風險，並非絕對存活率。HR=0.5 意指任一時刻處理組的瞬時風險為對照組的 50%。HR is the ratio of hazards — relative risk, not absolute survival. HR=0.5 means the treatment's instantaneous risk is 50% of control's at every time.

Q4. KM 曲線交叉時 log-rank 會？

A. 檢定力提升Gain power
B. 檢定力大降Lose power substantially
C. 永遠拒絕 H₀Always reject H₀
D. 沒有影響No effect
log-rank 對 PH 違反敏感，曲線交叉表示早晚期 HR 不同，會在加總時抵消，導致檢定力大降。改用 RMST 或加權 log-rank。Log-rank assumes PH; crossing curves mean early/late HRs differ and cancel out, killing power. Use RMST or weighted log-rank instead.
