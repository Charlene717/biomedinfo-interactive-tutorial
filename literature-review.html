文獻檢索與系統性回顧

透徹的文獻檢索是研究的起點——它告訴你已知什麼、爭議在哪、以及自己的位置。

A thorough literature search is where research begins — it tells you what is known, what is contested, and where your study sits.

概覽

為什麼專業檢索與「隨便 Google」不同？

生醫文獻每年新增 100 萬篇以上 (PubMed 收錄 3,500 萬 +)，靠關鍵字隨意搜尋會：(1) 漏掉重要的同義詞、(2) 被演算法個人化結果偏倚、(3) 無法重現。專業檢索強調 透明、完整、可重現，能讓你的綜述或 Meta 分析經得起同儕審查。

Over 1 million biomedical papers are added each year (PubMed indexes 35M+). Casual keyword searches will (1) miss critical synonyms, (2) be biased by personalization, and (3) be irreproducible. Professional searching emphasizes transparency, completeness, reproducibility — letting your review or meta-analysis survive peer review.

💡
系統性回顧的金科玉律：把檢索式逐字記錄、註明檢索日期、儲存原始 .nbib 匯出檔——任何人都能在 6 個月後重現同一組結果。 Golden rule of systematic reviews: Record the full query verbatim, note the search date, save the raw .nbib export — anyone should be able to reproduce the same hits six months later.

檢索語法

二、MeSH、布林邏輯與欄位標記

MeSH (Medical Subject Headings) 是美國國家醫學圖書館維護的層級式醫學主題詞彙，能解決同義詞問題：搜尋 "Heart Attack" 自動涵蓋 "Myocardial Infarction" 的所有變體。

布林運算符：

  • AND：兩者皆需出現（交集）
  • OR：任一出現即可（聯集，常用於同義詞群）
  • NOT：排除特定詞（謹慎使用，可能誤刪相關文章）

常用 PubMed 欄位標記：[MeSH]、[tiab]（title/abstract）、[au]（作者）、[ta]（期刊）、[dp]（出版年）、[pt]（文獻類型）。

MeSH (Medical Subject Headings) is the NLM's hierarchical thesaurus that solves the synonym problem: searching "Heart Attack" automatically expands to all "Myocardial Infarction" variants.

Boolean operators:

  • AND — both must appear (intersection)
  • OR — either appears (union, for synonym groups)
  • NOT — excludes a term (use with care — may drop relevant articles)

Useful PubMed field tags: [MeSH], [tiab] (title/abstract), [au], [ta] (journal), [dp] (publication year), [pt] (publication type).

# Question: Does metformin improve survival in T2DM patients with cancer?
("Metformin"[MeSH] OR "metformin"[tiab] OR "Glucophage"[tiab])
AND
("Diabetes Mellitus, Type 2"[MeSH] OR "T2DM"[tiab])
AND
("Neoplasms"[MeSH] OR "cancer"[tiab] OR "tumor"[tiab])
AND
("Survival"[MeSH] OR "mortality"[tiab] OR "prognosis"[tiab])
AND
("2015"[dp]:"2026"[dp])
AND
(English[la])
NOT
("Case Reports"[pt] OR "Editorial"[pt])
library(rentrez)

query <- '("Metformin"[MeSH]) AND ("Neoplasms"[MeSH]) AND ("Survival"[MeSH])'
res   <- entrez_search(db="pubmed", term=query, retmax=500, use_history=TRUE)

# Pull metadata in batches of 100
ids     <- res$ids
records <- entrez_fetch(db="pubmed", id=ids, rettype="medline", retmode="text")
writeLines(records, "metformin_cancer.nbib")
cat("Hits:", res$count, "\n")
from Bio import Entrez

Entrez.email = "you@uni.edu"  # NCBI requires email
query = '("Metformin"[MeSH]) AND ("Neoplasms"[MeSH]) AND ("Survival"[MeSH])'

handle = Entrez.esearch(db="pubmed", term=query, retmax=500)
res    = Entrez.read(handle); handle.close()
ids    = res["IdList"]
print(f"Hits: {res['Count']}")

# Fetch full records
h = Entrez.efetch(db="pubmed", id=ids, rettype="medline", retmode="text")
with open("metformin_cancer.nbib", "w") as f:
    f.write(h.read())
PRISMA 流程

三、PRISMA 2020 流程

PRISMA (Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses) 是系統性回顧的報告準則。其流程圖一定要呈現四個階段的文獻數量：

PRISMA is the reporting guideline for systematic reviews and meta-analyses. The flow diagram must always show counts for these four stages:

Identification

所有資料庫初始檢出的文獻總數（去重前）。

Total records retrieved from all databases (before deduplication).

Screening

去重後、依標題/摘要篩選；記錄排除原因。

After deduplication, screen by title/abstract; record exclusion reasons.

Eligibility

全文評估；每篇排除文獻都要寫明原因（PICO 不符、語言、研究類型等）。

Full-text assessment; every exclusion must have a documented reason (PICO mismatch, language, study type, etc.).

Included

最終納入質性綜述與/或定量合成（Meta 分析）的研究數。

Final number of studies included in qualitative synthesis and/or quantitative synthesis (meta-analysis).

💡
實用工具：Rayyan、Covidence 提供雙人盲審篩選介面；EndNote / Zotero 可去重；ROBIS / AMSTAR-2 用來評估綜述自身的偏倚風險。 Useful tools: Rayyan and Covidence offer blinded dual-screening; EndNote/Zotero handle deduplication; ROBIS/AMSTAR-2 assess the review's own risk of bias.

Q1. 為什麼系統性回顧通常要至少檢索 3 個資料庫？

A. 不同資料庫收錄期刊有差異，避免遺漏Different databases index different journals; avoid omissions
B. 這樣可以提高 IFIt boosts the journal's impact factor
C. 3 是 PRISMA 規定的最小整數3 is required by PRISMA
D. 減少 publication biasReduces publication bias
每個資料庫收錄期刊與會議摘要不同（如 Embase 比 PubMed 多歐洲期刊），多源檢索是「完整性」要件。Each database covers different journals/conferences (e.g., Embase has more EU journals than PubMed). Multi-source searching is essential for completeness.

Q2. PubMed [tiab] 代表？

A. 作者所屬機構Author affiliation
B. 標題與摘要 (title & abstract)Title & abstract
C. 期刊名稱Journal title
D. 出版年份Publication year
[tiab] = title + abstract，是同時在標題與摘要中檢索文字的常用標記。[tiab] searches both the title and abstract fields.

Q3. 同義詞之間應該用哪個布林運算符？

A. AND
B. OR
C. NOT
D. XOR（互斥或）XOR (exclusive or)
OR 取聯集，用來連結同義詞；AND 用在不同概念群之間（如「腫瘤」AND「治療」AND「存活」）。OR unions synonyms within a concept group; AND combines different concept groups (e.g., "tumor" AND "treatment" AND "survival").

Q4. PRISMA 四階段的順序？

A. Screening → Identification → Eligibility → Included
B. Identification → Screening → Eligibility → Included
C. Eligibility → Identification → Included → Screening
D. Included → Screening → Eligibility → Identification
PRISMA 流程是漏斗式縮小：Identification（初始檢出） → Screening（去重+標題篩選） → Eligibility（全文評估） → Included（最終納入）。PRISMA is a funnel: Identification (initial retrieval) → Screening (dedup + title/abstract) → Eligibility (full-text) → Included (final synthesis).
