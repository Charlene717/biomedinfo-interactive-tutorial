補充說明 · Notes

Step 5 · 樣本量公式為近似

頁面內模擬器與公式皆使用常態近似 (z-score)，未考慮 t 分配的自由度修正。對小樣本 (n < 30) 應改用 t 分配或 simulation-based 樣本量；對非二元 outcome（如比例、生存）建議使用 pwr 或 G*Power 的專屬公式。

The simulator uses normal approximations (z-scores) without t-distribution adjustment. For small n (< 30) prefer t-based or simulation-based sample size; for non-Gaussian outcomes use pwr or G*Power's dedicated formulas.

補充說明 · Notes

Step 7 · GWAS 5×10⁻⁸ 的真實依據

教程簡化為「人類基因組 ~10⁶ 個獨立 LD block」，這是普及說法。Pe'er et al. 2008 給出的數字實際為「常見變異約 1.2×10⁶ 個獨立檢定」，且僅針對歐裔人群；其他族群（如非洲裔，LD 較短）獨立檢定數更多。新一代 GWAS / WGS 研究常使用更嚴格的閾值（如 5×10⁻⁹）。

The tutorial simplifies to "human genome has ~10⁶ independent LD blocks." Pe'er et al. 2008 actually reports ~1.2×10⁶ for common variants, and only for Europeans; populations with shorter LD (e.g., African) have more independent tests. Newer GWAS / WGS often use stricter thresholds (e.g., 5×10⁻⁹).

補充說明 · Notes

Step 8 · FHIR R4 vs R5 遷移現況

教程內互通範例多以 FHIR R4 (2019 Normative) 為主，因其為當前 US Core IG 與 ONC USCDI v3 的基礎；FHIR R5 雖於 2023-03 發布，但臨床系統實裝速度極慢，多數 EHR 廠商與 HL7 Argonaut Project 仍以 R4 為主流。學習者應留意：(a) R5 的 SubscriptionTopic 取代 R4 的 Subscription criteria-as-string；(b) R5 移除 MedicationStatement.context 等欄位；(c) 跨機構交換目前仍應以 R4 為交付目標，R5 視為前瞻學習。

The tutorial's interoperability examples primarily use FHIR R4 (Normative 2019) since it underpins US Core IG and ONC USCDI v3. FHIR R5 was released 2023-03 but EHR vendor adoption lags; HL7 Argonaut Project and most production EHRs remain R4-centric. Note: (a) R5's SubscriptionTopic replaces R4's criteria-as-string Subscription; (b) R5 drops fields like MedicationStatement.context; (c) cross-institution exchange should still target R4 today, treating R5 as forward-looking study.

補充說明 · Notes

Step 9 · OMOP CDM v5.4 vs v6 狀態

教程示例 ETL 採用 OHDSI OMOP CDM v5.4 (2022 release)，包含 DRUG_EXPOSURE / CONDITION_ERA / OBSERVATION_PERIOD 等核心表格。CDM v6 草案曾於 2018 提出（將日期欄位升級為 datetime、調整 PERSON.death_datetime 至 DEATH 表），但 OHDSI 社群因下游工具相容性問題（ATLAS、Achilles、PatientLevelPrediction 等仍以 v5.x 為主）暫緩 v6 全面採用。截至 2025 Q4，v5.4 仍是事實標準；v6 GA 時程未定，建議以 v5.4 為交付目標。

Tutorial ETL examples follow OHDSI OMOP CDM v5.4 (2022 release) with DRUG_EXPOSURE / CONDITION_ERA / OBSERVATION_PERIOD core tables. CDM v6 draft (2018) proposed upgrading date fields to datetime and moving PERSON.death_datetime to a DEATH table, but downstream tool compatibility (ATLAS, Achilles, PatientLevelPrediction still v5.x-centric) stalled adoption. As of 2025 Q4, v5.4 remains the de facto standard; v6 GA timeline is undetermined — target v5.4 for delivery.

補充說明 · Notes

Step 10 · MIMIC-IV CITI 與 PhysioNet 認證

Johnson et al. 2023 (Scientific Data) 發布 MIMIC-IV v2.2 後，PhysioNet 取用門檻包含：(a) 完成 CITI Program "Data or Specimens Only Research" (DSOR) 模組（非完整 Human Subjects Research，但仍需 1.5–2 小時）；(b) 簽署 PhysioNet Credentialed Health Data Use Agreement；(c) 提交 referee 推薦（通常為 PI 或學術導師）。實務常見錯誤：誤用 CITI "Biomedical Research" 模組（不被接受），或下載後將資料置於公共雲儲存 (S3 public bucket)（違反 DUA）。MIMIC-IV-ED 與 MIMIC-IV-Note 需另外申請存取，雖然共用 CITI 訓練。

Following Johnson et al. 2023 (Scientific Data) release of MIMIC-IV v2.2, PhysioNet access requires: (a) completing CITI Program "Data or Specimens Only Research" (DSOR) module — NOT the full Human Subjects module, but still 1.5–2 hours; (b) signing the PhysioNet Credentialed Health Data Use Agreement; (c) submitting a referee endorsement (typically PI or academic mentor). Common mistakes: completing the wrong CITI "Biomedical Research" module (rejected); placing downloaded data in public cloud storage (S3 public bucket) which violates the DUA. MIMIC-IV-ED and MIMIC-IV-Note require separate access requests despite sharing the CITI training.

補充說明 · Notes

Step 11 · ICD-10 vs ICD-11 過渡

教程的診斷編碼示例仍以 ICD-10-CM (美國臨床版) 與 ICD-10 WHO 為主。WHO 已於 2022-01 正式啟用 ICD-11，但全球採用緩慢：美國 CMS 尚未公告 ICD-11-CM 開發時程，預估至 2027–2030 才會強制過渡；歐盟成員國採用時程不一（德國、義大利已試點）；台灣健保署目前仍維持 ICD-10-CM/PCS 編碼。實務影響：(a) ICD-11 採用線性化（linearization）+ 後組合（postcoordination）使 code 可變長度；(b) 新增「擴展碼」(extension codes) 與「叢集」(clustering) 概念，與 ICD-10 的階層碼結構不直接 1:1 映射；(c) 跨版本對照建議用 WHO 官方 ICD-10 ↔ ICD-11 mapping table，避免自製對照表。

Tutorial diagnosis coding examples still focus on ICD-10-CM (US clinical) and ICD-10 WHO. WHO officially launched ICD-11 in 2022-01 but global adoption is slow: US CMS has not announced an ICD-11-CM development timeline, with mandatory transition estimated 2027–2030; EU member states adopt at different paces (Germany, Italy piloted); Taiwan NHIA still uses ICD-10-CM/PCS. Practical impact: (a) ICD-11 uses linearization + postcoordination giving variable-length codes; (b) introduces "extension codes" and "clustering" concepts not directly 1:1 with ICD-10 hierarchy; (c) cross-version mapping should use WHO's official ICD-10 ↔ ICD-11 mapping table — do not roll your own.

補充說明 · Notes

Step 12 · 臨床 NLP 領域漂移 (Wornow 2023)

教程中介紹 ClinicalBERT、BioBERT、GatorTron 等臨床預訓練模型時，未充分強調 領域漂移 (domain shift) 問題。Wornow et al. 2023 (NEJM AI) 系統性分析顯示：(a) 在 MIMIC-III (Beth Israel ICU 2001–2012 資料) 訓練的模型，部署至同院 2020 後資料時 F1 可下降 10–15 個百分點；(b) 跨醫院遷移更嚴重（特別是書寫風格差異、模板化結構差異）；(c) 多數已發表 clinical NLP 模型缺乏外部驗證，建議 transfer learning 後仍需在目標院別 fine-tune 並計算 calibration shift。即便 LLM (GPT-4/Med-PaLM 2) 在 zero-shot 表現亮眼，仍對 ICD coding 與 medication extraction 任務存在系統性偏誤。

When introducing ClinicalBERT, BioBERT, GatorTron and other clinical pretrained models, the tutorial under-emphasizes domain shift. Wornow et al. 2023 (NEJM AI) systematic analysis shows: (a) models trained on MIMIC-III (Beth Israel ICU 2001–2012) drop 10–15 F1 points when deployed on same-hospital post-2020 data; (b) cross-hospital transfer is worse (note-writing style, template structure differences); (c) most published clinical NLP models lack external validation — after transfer learning, fine-tune on target-site data and check calibration shift. Even LLMs (GPT-4/Med-PaLM 2) with strong zero-shot performance show systematic bias on ICD coding and medication extraction tasks.

補充說明 · Notes

Step 13 · SDOH Z-code 低估率 (Truong 2020)

教程提及社會決定因素 (Social Determinants of Health, SDOH) 在 ICD-10 中以 Z55–Z65 系列 Z-codes 編碼（住房不穩、糧食不安全、社會孤立等），但嚴重低報是已知問題。Truong et al. 2020 (Population Health Management) 全國分析發現：(a) 全美 Medicare/Medicaid claims 中僅 1.6% 病患有任何 Z-code SDOH 紀錄；(b) Z59.x (住房問題) 編碼率不到實際盛行率的 5%；(c) 編碼率在 safety-net hospital 較高但仍偏低；原因包括缺乏結算誘因、護理人員與社工的編碼工作流斷層、provider 訓練不足。實務建議：(a) 若以 Z-code 推估 SDOH 暴露會嚴重低估；(b) 改採 EHR 自由文本 NLP 萃取（如 SHC SDOH Toolkit）或社區層級指標 (ADI, SVI) 連結；(c) 整體 sensitivity 通常 <20%。

The tutorial mentions Social Determinants of Health (SDOH) coded as Z55–Z65 ICD-10 Z-codes (housing instability, food insecurity, social isolation) but severe under-coding is a known issue. Truong et al. 2020 (Population Health Management) national analysis found: (a) only 1.6% of US Medicare/Medicaid claims had any Z-code SDOH entry; (b) Z59.x (housing) coding captures <5% of actual prevalence; (c) rates are higher at safety-net hospitals but still low — driven by lack of reimbursement incentive, broken handoffs between nursing/social work coding workflows, and inadequate provider training. Practical guidance: (a) inferring SDOH exposure from Z-codes alone severely underestimates; (b) use EHR free-text NLP extraction (e.g., SHC SDOH Toolkit) or area-level proxies (ADI, SVI) linked to address; (c) overall sensitivity is typically <20%.
