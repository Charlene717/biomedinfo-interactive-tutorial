科學寫作與發表

IMRaD 結構、期刊選擇、同儕審查、預印本、作者貢獻——把研究變成可流通的學術產出。

IMRaD structure, journal choice, peer review, preprints, authorship — turn your research into a circulating scientific product.

概覽

科學寫作的目的

研究做完不等於完成。論文的目的是讓陌生讀者在 30 分鐘內理解你做了什麼、為什麼重要、結論是否可信。三個核心原則：

  • 清晰 (Clarity)：主動句、短句、定義術語
  • 誠實 (Honesty)：不誇大、報告限制與否定結果
  • 可重現 (Reproducibility)：讓他人能跟你的步驟做出相同結果

Doing the research isn't enough. A paper exists so a stranger can understand in 30 minutes what you did, why it matters, and whether to trust the conclusion. Three principles:

  • Clarity: active voice, short sentences, define terms
  • Honesty: don't overstate; report limitations and negative results
  • Reproducibility: readers can reproduce your steps
IMRaD 結構

一、IMRaD 標準結構

Introduction

採「漏斗」結構：寬→窄。第一段：領域背景 + 重要性；中段：既有知識缺口；最後段：本研究的目標與假設（呼應 PICO）。長度約 3-4 段。

"Funnel" structure: broad → narrow. Para 1: field + significance; middle: gap in knowledge; final para: this study's aim & hypothesis (echo PICO). 3-4 paragraphs.

Methods

夠詳細到能讓他人重做——資料來源、樣本特徵、處理方法、統計分析、軟體版本、code/data 可取得性。Subsections 常包括：Study design、Participants、Data sources、Statistical analysis。

Detailed enough to repeat — data sources, sample characteristics, processing, statistical analysis, software versions, code/data availability. Subsections: Study design, Participants, Data sources, Statistical analysis.

Results

只報告結果、不解讀。從整體 cohort 特徵（Table 1）→ 主要結果（Figure 1-3）→ 次要 / 敏感度分析。每個 figure 配獨立段落。所有 p 值搭配效應量與 CI。

Report results only — no interpretation. Cohort characteristics (Table 1) → primary results (Figure 1-3) → secondary / sensitivity. One paragraph per figure. Every p with effect size + CI.

Discussion

「倒漏斗」結構：窄→寬。第一段：主要發現摘要；中段：與既有文獻對照、可能機制；倒數第二段：限制 (Limitations)；最後：意義與未來方向。

"Inverted funnel": narrow → broad. Para 1: main findings summary; middle: comparison with prior work, potential mechanisms; penultimate: Limitations; final: implications and future directions.

期刊選擇

二、期刊選擇

維度 考量
範圍 期刊範圍是否涵蓋你的研究主題？讀「Aims & Scope」與近 6 個月文章類型。Does the scope cover your topic? Read "Aims & Scope" and articles from the last 6 months.
受眾 你希望誰讀到？臨床醫師（NEJM、JAMA）vs 基礎研究 (Cell、Nature) vs 跨域 (PLOS Biology) vs 領域專業 (Bioinformatics、Genome Biology)。Who do you want as readers? Clinicians (NEJM/JAMA) vs basic science (Cell/Nature) vs interdisciplinary (PLOS Biology) vs domain (Bioinformatics, Genome Biology).
影響因子 IF 是粗略指標但有限制：能反映期刊整體被引用率，不代表你的文章品質。也參考 CiteScore、SNIP、SJR。A rough indicator with caveats: reflects overall citations, not your article's quality. Also consult CiteScore, SNIP, SJR.
OA 模式 Gold OA（作者付 APC）、Green OA（自存 preprint）、Diamond OA（免費）。NIH / 多家機構強制要求公開存取。Gold OA (APC), Green OA (self-archive preprint), Diamond OA (free). NIH and many funders mandate OA.
審查時間 中位審查時間（很多期刊已公佈）；deadline 緊迫時優先考量。Median time-to-decision (many journals publish this); critical if you have deadlines.
掠奪性風險 避開 Beall's List、Cabells Predatory Reports 上的期刊。同儕審查不嚴、收費快、來歷不明。Avoid journals on Beall's List or Cabells' Predatory Reports — sketchy review, fast fees, dubious provenance.
同儕審查

三、同儕審查策略

📨 提交準備

Cover letter（強調貢獻 + 重要性）；建議 / 排除審稿人；遵守 author guidelines（字數、格式、checklist 如 STROBE / CONSORT / ARRIVE）。

Cover letter (highlight contribution + significance); suggest/exclude reviewers; follow author guidelines (length, format, checklists like STROBE/CONSORT/ARRIVE).

🔄 決定信解讀

Accept / Minor revision / Major revision / Reject & resubmit / Reject。Major 並不可怕，是讓研究變得更好的機會。回覆要逐點 (point-by-point)。

Accept / Minor / Major / Reject & resubmit / Reject. Major revision isn't bad — it's an opportunity. Reply point-by-point.

📝 回應信

結構：Reviewer comment（原文引用） → Our response（如何回應）→ Changes in manuscript（具體修改處及頁碼）。即使不同意審稿人，也要禮貌、有依據地辯駁。

Structure: Reviewer comment (quoted verbatim) → Our response → Changes in manuscript (with location). Disagreeing? Be polite and back it with evidence.

⚠️ 幾項禁忌

不要：忽略某條評語、惱怒回應、同時投多家（duplicate submission）、不對 reviewer 表達感謝。Salami publishing（一個資料拆好幾篇）也是嚴重學術倫理問題。

Don't: ignore comments, respond in anger, simultaneous submission (duplicate), forget to thank reviewers. Salami publishing (slicing one dataset across many papers) is a serious ethical issue.

作者貢獻

四、作者標準與貢獻

ICMJE 四項作者準則（必須全部符合）：

  1. 對研究構思、設計、資料取得或分析有實質貢獻
  2. 撰寫或關鍵性修訂稿件
  3. 同意最終投稿版本
  4. 對研究內容負責，能解答相關問題

CRediT 14 項分類：Conceptualization、Methodology、Software、Validation、Formal analysis、Investigation、Resources、Data curation、Writing - original draft、Writing - review & editing、Visualization、Supervision、Project administration、Funding acquisition。每位作者明確標示貢獻。

ORCID（Open Researcher and Contributor ID）：永久學者身份碼，全球通用。

ICMJE four authorship criteria (all must be met):

  1. Substantial contribution to conception, design, data acquisition, or analysis
  2. Drafting or critically revising the manuscript
  3. Final approval of the version to be published
  4. Accountability for all aspects of the work

CRediT 14 roles: Conceptualization, Methodology, Software, Validation, Formal analysis, Investigation, Resources, Data curation, Writing - original draft, Writing - review & editing, Visualization, Supervision, Project administration, Funding acquisition. Each author's contributions are explicit.

ORCID (Open Researcher and Contributor ID): a persistent global researcher identifier.

預印本

五、預印本與開放科學

Preprint：未經同儕審查的研究稿件，公開於 bioRxiv / medRxiv / arXiv 等平台。優點：

  • 第一時間佔據優先權
  • 立即取得社群回饋
  • 研究成果不被審查流程拖延
  • 計算學者可在預印本上引用最新方法

注意事項：（1）大部分主流期刊允許 preprint，但少數仍視為「事先發表」；投稿前查詢期刊 preprint policy。（2）預印本仍可後來於同儕審查期刊出版，DOI 不衝突。

開放科學三柱：Open Access（論文）+ Open Data（資料）+ Open Code（程式碼）。FAIR + 預先註冊 + 註冊報告 (Registered Reports) 是趨勢。

Preprint: a manuscript shared publicly before peer review on bioRxiv / medRxiv / arXiv. Benefits:

  • Immediate priority claim
  • Real-time community feedback
  • Output not held hostage by review delays
  • Computational scientists can cite cutting-edge methods

Caveats: (1) Most mainstream journals allow preprints, but a few still consider them "prior publication" — check journal policy first. (2) Preprints can later be peer-review-published; DOIs don't conflict.

Three pillars of open science: Open Access (paper) + Open Data + Open Code. FAIR + pre-registration + Registered Reports are the direction of travel.

📝 隨堂測驗

Q1. IMRaD 中「D」是？

A. Data
B. Discussion
C. Design
D. Diagnosis
IMRaD = Introduction / Methods / Results / and Discussion，是生醫論文的核心結構。IMRaD = Introduction / Methods / Results / and Discussion — the core biomedical paper structure.

Q2. 觀察性研究的指引？

A. CONSORT
B. STROBE
C. PRISMA
D. ARRIVE
STROBE 是 cohort、case-control、cross-sectional 研究的標準報告指引。CONSORT 為 RCT、PRISMA 為系統性回顧、ARRIVE 為動物實驗。STROBE covers cohort, case-control, cross-sectional. CONSORT = RCTs; PRISMA = SRs; ARRIVE = animal research.

Q3. 下列哪項不是 ICMJE 作者準則？

A. 對研究有實質貢獻Substantial contribution
B. 參與撰寫或關鍵修訂Drafting / critical revision
C. 提供經費支持Provided funding
D. 同意最終版本並負責Final approval and accountability
僅提供經費不能列為作者（應放 Acknowledgements）。ICMJE 四準則為：貢獻、撰寫/修訂、同意版本、負責。Funding alone doesn't make you an author (acknowledge instead). The four ICMJE criteria are: contribution, drafting/revision, final approval, accountability.

Q4. 預印本主要價值？

A. 取代同儕審查Replaces peer review
B. 快速建立優先權與取得社群回饋Quickly establish priority and gather feedback
C. 省去寫期刊版本Skip the journal version
D. 增加 IFBoost impact factor
預印本讓研究在審查期間就被引用、批評、修正。它不取代同儕審查，而是補位於審查之前。Preprints surface research during peer review for early citation, critique, and refinement — they don't replace peer review.
