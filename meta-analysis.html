Meta 分析與系統性回顧

把多項既有研究的證據以統計方法綜合，得到效應的更精確估計與一致性評估。

Statistically combine evidence from multiple studies to obtain a more precise effect estimate and assess consistency.

概覽

Meta 分析的本質

Meta-analysis 是把多個獨立研究的效應量「加權平均」的統計方法，常作為系統性回顧的定量合成步驟。它的優勢是：

  • 提高檢定力：合併樣本，能偵測單一研究無法揭露的小效應
  • 提高精確度：縮窄信賴區間
  • 探索異質性：各研究效應一致還是分歧？
  • 偵測偏倚：發表偏倚、小樣本研究效應

證據等級金字塔頂端：高品質系統性回顧/Meta-analysis > RCT > cohort > case-control > case series > expert opinion。

Meta-analysis is the statistical pooling of effect sizes from independent studies, typically the quantitative arm of a systematic review. Strengths:

  • Greater power — pool subjects to detect small effects single studies miss
  • Greater precision — narrower CIs
  • Heterogeneity probe — are studies consistent or divergent?
  • Bias detection — publication bias, small-study effects

Top of the evidence pyramid: well-conducted SR/MA > RCT > cohort > case-control > case series > expert opinion.

模型選擇

一、固定效應 vs 隨機效應

— 固定效應 隨機效應
假設 所有研究的「真效應」皆相同All studies share one true effect 真效應在研究間有分布True effects vary across studies (distribution)
權重來源 僅 within-study varianceWithin-study variance only within + between-study variance (τ²)Within + between (τ²)
信賴區間 較窄Narrower 較寬（保守）Wider (conservative)
適用 研究設計、族群極相似時Studies very similar 大部分臨床 / 流行病學 MetaMost clinical / epi metas
💡
實用建議：當你不確定該用哪個模型時，預設用隨機效應——更保守、更貼近現實世界研究設計差異。Cochrane 2025 也建議優先用隨機效應。 Practical advice: When unsure, default to random effects — more conservative, more realistic. Cochrane 2025 also recommends random effects as the default.
森林圖

二、森林圖的解讀

森林圖是 Meta 分析的標準視覺化，每行代表一項研究：

  • 方塊大小反映該研究的權重（通常正比於 1/variance）
  • 方塊位置是效應點估計
  • 橫線是 95% CI
  • 底部菱形代表合併估計與其 CI，中心 = 合併效應，寬度 = CI
  • 垂直線（OR=1 或 SMD=0）是「沒有效應」線

The forest plot is meta-analysis's signature graphic. Each row is a study:

  • Square size reflects study weight (typically ∝ 1/variance)
  • Square position is the point estimate
  • Horizontal line is the 95% CI
  • The diamond at the bottom is the pooled estimate (center) and its CI (width)
  • Vertical line (OR=1 or SMD=0) is the "no-effect" line

異質性

三、異質性與發表偏倚

📊 I² 異質性百分比

研究間變異佔總變異的比例。Higgins 2003 經驗法則：
• 0-40% 低
• 30-60% 中
• 50-90% 大
• 75-100% 極大

I² 高時應做次群組分析或meta-regression 找原因，或考慮不合併。

Fraction of total variation due to between-study heterogeneity. Higgins 2003 rule of thumb:
• 0-40% low
• 30-60% moderate
• 50-90% substantial
• 75-100% considerable

High I² → run subgroup analysis or meta-regression; consider not pooling.

📐 τ² 研究間變異

絕對尺度的研究間真效應變異數。常用 REML 或 DerSimonian-Laird 估計。τ² → √τ² 可用「預測區間 (PI)」呈現「新研究的真效應可能落在哪」。

The between-study variance on the absolute scale. Typically estimated by REML or DerSimonian-Laird. Use √τ² to derive a prediction interval (PI) — "where would a new study's true effect lie?"

📊 Funnel plot

效應量 (x 軸) vs 精確度 / SE (y 軸)。對稱漏斗形 = 無偏；單側缺塊 = 可能有發表偏倚（小規模、效應小的負面結果未發表）。配合 Egger 檢定。

Effect size (x) vs precision/SE (y). Symmetric funnel = unbiased; missing side = potential publication bias (small null studies unpublished). Pair with Egger's test.

🛠 校正方法

Trim-and-fill、PET-PEESE、Selection models。提醒：這些方法都是診斷+敏感度分析，無法完全「修復」缺失研究。

Trim-and-fill, PET-PEESE, selection models. Caveat: these are diagnostics + sensitivity, not full repairs for missing studies.

證據評級

四、GRADE 證據評級

GRADE (Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation) 是評估證據品質的國際標準，從 4 個等級（High / Moderate / Low / Very Low）描述對效應估計的信心度。

RCT 起始為 High，可被 5 因素下調：

  1. 研究偏倚風險 (Risk of bias)
  2. 結果不一致 (Inconsistency, I² 高)
  3. 間接性 (Indirectness, PICO 不完全對應)
  4. 精確度不足 (Imprecision, CI 過寬)
  5. 發表偏倚 (Publication bias)

觀察性研究起始為 Low，可被 3 因素上調：大效應、劑量-反應、調整後仍存在效應。

GRADE rates evidence in four tiers (High / Moderate / Low / Very Low). RCTs start High and can be downgraded by:

  1. Risk of bias
  2. Inconsistency (high I²)
  3. Indirectness (PICO mismatch)
  4. Imprecision (wide CI)
  5. Publication bias

Observational studies start Low and can be upgraded by large effect, dose-response, or residual effect after adjustment.

程式碼

實作範例

library(meta)
# Binary outcomes (RR, OR, risk diff)
m <- metabin(event.e=df$e_t, n.e=df$n_t,
             event.c=df$e_c, n.c=df$n_c,
             studlab=df$study,
             sm="OR", method="MH", common=TRUE, random=TRUE)
summary(m)
forest(m, label.e="Treatment", label.c="Control")
funnel(m); metabias(m, method="linreg")
library(metafor)

# Compute log OR + variance
dat <- escalc(measure="OR", ai=tpos, bi=tneg,
              ci=cpos, di=cneg, data=df)

# Random-effects with REML
res <- rma(yi, vi, data=dat, method="REML")
summary(res)        # tau^2, I^2, pooled OR
forest(res, atransf=exp, slab=dat$study)
funnel(res); regtest(res, model="lm")

# Meta-regression by year
rma(yi, vi, mods=~year, data=dat)
import PythonMeta as PM

stng = {
  "datatype":"CATE",    # categorical (event counts)
  "models":"Random", "algorithm":"DL",
  "effect":"OR"
}
m = PM.Meta()
m.datatype="CATE"; m.data = PM.readfile("data.csv")
m.subgroup=None
result = m.meta(stng)
m.forest(stng, result); m.funnel(stng, result)

📝 隨堂測驗

Q1. I² = 80% 怎麼解讀？

A. 80% 研究效應為陽性80% of studies are positive
B. 大幅異質性——研究間真效應差異顯著Substantial heterogeneity — true effects vary considerably
C. 合併效應有 80% 信心80% confidence in pooled effect
D. 80% 樣本量已收集80% of subjects recruited
I² 是「研究間變異」佔總變異的比例。80% 顯示真效應在研究間差異顯著，需做次群組或 meta-regression。I² is the fraction of total variation due to heterogeneity. 80% indicates substantial between-study variance; run subgroup analysis or meta-regression.

Q2. 漏斗圖非對稱暗示？

A. 大樣本問題Issues with large samples
B. 可能存在發表偏倚或小規模研究效應Possible publication bias or small-study effects
C. 隨機效應模型錯誤Wrong random-effects model
D. 研究數不足Too few studies
漏斗圖橫軸效應、縱軸精確度，非對稱常見原因是小規模、效應陰性的研究未發表。配合 Egger 檢定。A funnel plot maps effect against precision. Asymmetry commonly arises from missing small null studies. Confirm with Egger's test.

Q3. 何時用隨機效應？

A. 研究的人口、處置、設計有合理差異時Studies vary in population, intervention, or design
B. 所有研究設計完全相同時All studies are identical
C. 資料是基因組學Data is genomic
D. 永遠不該用Never use it
當不確定時 Cochrane 建議預設隨機效應，因現實研究幾乎都有差異。固定效應假設過嚴。Cochrane recommends random effects as the default, because real-world studies almost always differ. Fixed-effect's assumption is too strict.

Q4. GRADE 中 RCT 起始評？

A. High
B. Moderate
C. Low
D. Very Low
RCT 因設計強度而起始於 High，可因偏倚風險、不一致、間接性、精確度、發表偏倚下調。RCTs start at High due to design strength, downgradable for risk of bias, inconsistency, indirectness, imprecision, publication bias.
