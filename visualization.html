ADVANCED

數據視覺化原則

圖表是濃縮的論證——選對圖、用對色、誠實標尺，能讓資料說真話。

A chart is a compressed argument — pick the right type, the right colors, and honest scales, and the data tells the truth.

概覽

為什麼好圖比好文字更有力？

審稿人、PI、聽眾的第一眼通常落在 Figure 1。Tufte 強調：「視覺呈現應最大化『資料-墨水比』，去掉裝飾、保留資訊」。生醫資訊圖表還承擔三個額外角色：

  • QC 工具：PCA、t-SNE、UMAP 在 Step 1 暴露 batch 與 outlier
  • 探索工具：volcano、heatmap、KM curve 揭示效應結構
  • 溝通工具：論文 Figure 1-6 是論證骨幹

Reviewers, PIs, and audiences look at Figure 1 first. Tufte's rule: "Maximize the data-ink ratio — remove decoration, keep information." In biomedical informatics, plots play three roles:

  • QC tool: PCA, t-SNE, UMAP expose batch and outliers at Step 1
  • Exploratory tool: volcano, heatmap, KM curve reveal effect structures
  • Communication tool: Figures 1-6 carry the paper's argument

色彩原則

二、色彩原則

🎨 連續色板 viridis 不用 rainbow

Rainbow (jet) 色板在感知亮度上不單調 → 視覺扭曲、色盲不友善。Viridis、magma、cividis 都是感知均勻 + 色盲友善的現代替代方案。Matplotlib 自 2.0、ggplot 自 3.0 內建 viridis。

Rainbow (jet) has non-monotonic perceptual brightness → visual distortion, colorblind-unfriendly. Viridis, magma, cividis are perceptually uniform + colorblind-safe. Matplotlib (≥2.0) and ggplot (≥3.0) include viridis by default.

👁️ 色盲安全比例

約 8% 男性、0.5% 女性有紅綠色盲。不要單獨依賴顏色傳達資訊——配合形狀、紋理、直接標籤。檢查工具：Coblis、Color Oracle。

~8% of men and 0.5% of women have red-green color blindness. Never rely on color alone — pair with shape, pattern, or direct labels. Tools: Coblis, Color Oracle.

↗️ 發散色板要置中

表達正負（如 log fold-change、相關係數）時使用 RdBu_r、coolwarm 等發散色板，並讓白色對齊到 0，否則會誤導效應方向。

For signed data (log FC, correlation), use diverging palettes (RdBu_r, coolwarm) with white aligned to 0 — otherwise the sign is visually misleading.

🚫 不要斷裂 y 軸

把 y 軸從 80 開始畫 long bar 會放大微小差異，是經典誤導手法。除非有強烈合理原因，y 軸應從 0 起。對極端值用對數刻度而非斷裂。

Starting y at 80 inflates tiny differences — a classic misleading trick. y should start at 0 unless strongly justified. For extreme ranges, use a log scale, not a break.

不要做的事

三、八個請勿模仿

❌ Dynamite bar

只畫 mean 與一條誤差線：完全隱藏分布、樣本量、離群值。改用 boxplot+jitter 或 violin。

Only shows mean and one error bar — hides distribution, n, outliers. Use boxplot + jitter or violin instead.

❌ 3D 圓餅 / 3D 長條

3D 視角扭曲面積與長度感知，違反「資料-墨水比」原則。永遠用 2D。

3D distorts area and length perception, violating data-ink ratio. Use 2D — always.

❌ 沒有 n 的 boxplot

10 vs 1000 樣本的 boxplot 看起來一樣寬。應在每組標註 n。

A 10-sample box and a 1000-sample box look identical. Annotate n.

❌ 連續資料用長條圖

長條圖只該用於計數或頻率。連續資料用 histogram / density。

Bars are for counts. Continuous data → histogram / density.

❌ 缺軸標 / 單位

「Expression」沒有單位是常見毛病。要清楚標 log2 CPM、TPM、Z-score、raw counts。

"Expression" without units is a common sin. Label log2 CPM, TPM, Z-score, or raw counts explicitly.

❌ 7 種以上線條色

人類短期記憶極限約 7 ± 2 個項目。多於 7 條線應分小圖 (facet)。

Working memory limit ≈ 7 ± 2. More than 7 lines → use small multiples (facets).

❌ 只秀 ** *** 沒效應量

p 值不能單獨衡量證據。也要呈現效應量 + CI（見 Step 6）。

p alone is no evidence. Show effect size + CI (see Step 6).

❌ 無 CI 的 KM

KM 缺 CI 與 at-risk table 等於缺乏不確定性資訊。生醫期刊現多要求 at-risk 表。

KM without CIs and at-risk tables hides uncertainty. Biomed journals increasingly require at-risk tables.

程式碼

實作範例

library(ggplot2); library(viridis); library(ggpubr)

# Boxplot + jitter + n labels (publication style)
ggplot(df, aes(group, value, fill=group)) +
  geom_violin(alpha=0.4, trim=FALSE) +
  geom_boxplot(width=0.15, outlier.shape=NA) +
  geom_jitter(width=0.1, alpha=0.5, size=0.7) +
  stat_compare_means(method="wilcox") +
  scale_fill_viridis_d(option="D") +
  labs(y="log2 TPM", x=NULL) +
  theme_classic(base_size=13)

# Volcano plot
ggplot(de, aes(log2FC, -log10(padj),
              color=padj<0.05 & abs(log2FC)>1)) +
  geom_point(size=0.8) +
  scale_color_manual(values=c("grey70","#d4a03c")) +
  geom_vline(xintercept=c(-1,1), lty=2) +
  geom_hline(yintercept=-log10(0.05), lty=2) +
  theme_classic()
import seaborn as sns, matplotlib.pyplot as plt

sns.set_theme(style="ticks", palette="viridis")

# Half-violin + box + strip
fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(5,4))
sns.violinplot(x="group", y="value", data=df, inner=None, alpha=0.4, ax=ax)
sns.boxplot(x="group", y="value", data=df, width=0.2, showfliers=False, ax=ax)
sns.stripplot(x="group", y="value", data=df, size=2, color="k", alpha=0.4, ax=ax)
ax.set(ylabel="log2 TPM", xlabel=""); sns.despine()

# Volcano plot
df["sig"] = (df["padj"]<0.05) & (df["logFC"].abs()>1)
plt.figure(figsize=(5,5))
plt.scatter(df.logFC, -np.log10(df.padj),
            c=df.sig.map({True:"#d4a03c",False:"grey"}),
            s=4, alpha=0.7)
plt.axhline(-np.log10(0.05), ls="--"); plt.axvline(1, ls="--"); plt.axvline(-1, ls="--")

📝 隨堂測驗

Q1. 連續資料分布用？

A. 3D 長條圖3D bar
B. histogram / violin / boxplot
C. 圓餅圖Pie chart
D. 堆疊長條Stacked bar
連續資料分布應呈現整體形狀（histogram/density）或四分位（boxplot/violin），不要只給平均值。Distributions need to show shape (histogram/density) or quartiles (boxplot/violin), not just a mean.

Q2. 為什麼避免 rainbow 色板？

A. 太鮮豔Too vibrant
B. 感知亮度不均勻、色盲不友善Non-uniform perceptual brightness, colorblind-unfriendly
C. 檔案太大Files too large
D. 只能用紅綠Only red/green works
Rainbow 在亮度（luminance）上不單調，會在數值大小感知上造成扭曲；對紅綠色盲也不友善。Viridis 是業界標準替代品。Rainbow's luminance is non-monotonic, distorting perceived magnitude and failing colorblind users. Viridis is the industry-standard alternative.

Q3. logFC 用哪種色板？

A. 順序色（如 viridis）Sequential (e.g., viridis)
B. 發散色 (diverging)，白色置 0Diverging, white at 0
C. 分類色 (Set1)Qualitative (Set1)
D. 黑白Black & white
logFC 有正負方向，要用 diverging 色板（如 RdBu_r）並讓白色對應到 0。否則正負方向會視覺混淆。logFC has sign — use a diverging palette (e.g., RdBu_r) centered at 0. Otherwise sign becomes visually misleading.

Q4. KM 曲線為什麼要 at-risk table？

A. 期刊規定Journal rule
B. 標示每個時間點還剩多少觀察值，揭露曲線可信度Shows how many subjects remain at each time — reveals curve reliability
C. 美觀Aesthetics
D. 取代 p 值Replaces p-value
at-risk 數量少時 KM 末端的估計波動極大，必須揭示給讀者。When the at-risk set is small the KM tail is highly variable — readers need to see that.
