STEP 1 / 9

研究設計與假說建立

所有後續分析的成敗，始於是否提出了一個可檢驗、有臨床或生物意義的研究問題。

The success of any downstream analysis begins with formulating a testable, clinically or biologically meaningful research question.

概覽

為什麼研究設計是最先要做的事？

生醫資訊研究的常見陷阱是「資料拿到手才開始想分析」。然而研究問題本身的結構決定了你可以使用哪些統計方法、所需樣本量、以及結論能推廣到哪些族群。在資料生成（無論是 wet lab 或公開資料庫下載）之前，先把研究設計想清楚，能避免：

  • p-hacking：因為沒有預設假說而事後挑選顯著結果
  • HARKing (Hypothesizing After the Results are Known)：先看結果再杜撰假說
  • 檢定力不足：發現效應後才驚覺樣本太少
  • 無法推論到目標族群：樣本與真正關心的族群結構不符

A common pitfall in biomedical informatics is "wait for the data, then think about analysis." But the structure of the research question itself determines which statistical methods you can use, how much sample you need, and which populations your conclusions generalize to. Settling the design before data generation (wet lab or public download) avoids:

  • p-hacking: cherry-picking significant results because no a priori hypothesis was set
  • HARKing (Hypothesizing After the Results are Known): inventing hypotheses post hoc
  • Underpowered studies: realizing only after observing an effect that the sample is too small
  • Non-generalizable conclusions: samples that don't reflect the target population
💡
核心原則：在打開任何資料、寫任何程式碼之前，先把研究問題、預期效應方向、主要分析變數寫下來——最好註冊到 OSF 或 ClinicalTrials.gov。這個動作把你的研究從「探索性」拉升到「確證性」的層次。 Core principle: Before opening any data or writing any code, write down the research question, expected effect direction, and primary analysis variables — preferably register them on OSF or ClinicalTrials.gov. This single act elevates your study from "exploratory" to "confirmatory."
核心框架

一、PICO 框架：建構可檢驗的問題

PICO 是循證醫學的核心問題模板，把模糊的「我想研究 X」拆解成 4 個明確的元件，讓問題立刻變得可檢索、可分析。

PICO is the foundational question template in evidence-based medicine. It decomposes a vague "I want to study X" into four explicit components, making the question instantly searchable and analyzable.

👤

P — Population / Patient

研究對象的明確定義：年齡、性別、疾病分期、診斷標準、共病排除條件。
範例：「第二期至第三期非小細胞肺腺癌，年齡 18-75 歲，無嚴重肝腎功能不全。」

Explicit definition of subjects: age, sex, disease stage, diagnostic criteria, comorbidity exclusions.
Example: "Stage II-III non-small-cell lung adenocarcinoma, ages 18-75, without severe hepatic or renal impairment."

💊

I — Intervention / Exposure

主要的處置、暴露、生物標記、基因突變或預測變數。
範例：「EGFR exon 19 deletion (突變組)」或「抽菸 ≥ 20 pack-years」。

Primary treatment, exposure, biomarker, mutation, or predictor.
Example: "EGFR exon 19 deletion (mutant group)" or "smoking ≥ 20 pack-years."

⚖️

C — Comparison / Control

對照組設計：標準治療、安慰劑、無暴露族群、wild-type 組。沒有對照組就無法談「差異」或「療效」。

Control group design: standard therapy, placebo, unexposed population, or wild-type group. Without a comparison, there is no "difference" or "treatment effect" to speak of.

🎯

O — Outcome

結果變數要在分析前定義清楚（primary vs. secondary endpoints）：存活時間、療效反應率、基因表達量、診斷準確率（AUC/敏感度/特異度）。

Define outcomes precisely before analysis (primary vs. secondary endpoints): survival time, response rate, gene expression, diagnostic accuracy (AUC/sensitivity/specificity).

⚠️
進階版本：PICOT 加入「T — Timeframe」（如 5 年存活率）；PICOS 加入「S — Study design」（如 RCT, cohort, case-control）。這些變體在系統性回顧與 Meta 分析中尤其重要。 Extended versions: PICOT adds "T — Timeframe" (e.g., 5-year survival); PICOS adds "S — Study design" (e.g., RCT, cohort, case-control). These variants are especially important in systematic reviews and meta-analyses.

如何選擇研究設計？

🌳 設計選擇流程

Q1→是否可以隨機分配介入？可以 → RCT (gold standard)；不可以 → 觀察性研究Can you randomize the intervention? Yes → RCT (gold standard); No → observational study
Q2→疾病很罕見？是 → Case-Control（從病例反查暴露）；否 → CohortIs the disease rare? Yes → Case-Control (from cases to exposure); No → Cohort
Q3→需要快速估算盛行率？是 → Cross-sectionalNeed quick prevalence estimate? Yes → Cross-sectional
Q4→想綜合既有文獻證據？是 → Systematic Review / Meta-analysisWant to synthesize existing evidence? Yes → Systematic Review / Meta-analysis
Q5→資料已存在於 TCGA/GEO/UK Biobank？是 → Secondary analysis (要特別注意 batch effect 與 cohort 特性)Data already exists in TCGA/GEO/UK Biobank? Yes → Secondary analysis (watch for batch effects and cohort characteristics)
程式碼

預先註冊與假說書面化

把假說、主要終點與分析計畫存成版本控管的檔案，是最務實的「研究設計鎖定」步驟。下例示範以 YAML / Quarto 記錄假說，並用 R 計算所需樣本量。

Storing the hypothesis, primary endpoint, and analysis plan as version-controlled files is the most practical way to "lock" a design. The example below logs the hypothesis in YAML/Quarto and computes sample size in R.

# Study protocol (version-controlled with Git)
study_id: "BMI-2026-001"
registration: "OSF.io/abcde"
hypothesis:
  primary: "EGFR-mutant NSCLC patients on osimertinib have longer PFS than on erlotinib."
  direction: "one-sided, osimertinib > erlotinib"
population:
  inclusion: ["Stage IIIB-IV NSCLC", "EGFR exon 19 del / L858R"]
  exclusion: ["prior EGFR-TKI", "ECOG > 2"]
intervention: "osimertinib 80mg QD"
comparator: "erlotinib 150mg QD"
primary_outcome: "Progression-free survival (months)"
secondary_outcomes: ["OS", "ORR", "adverse events grade ≥ 3"]
sample_size: 220
analysis_plan: "Cox PH adjusted for age, sex, smoking. Log-rank for K-M."
# Sample size calculation for survival study
library(survival)
library(powerSurvEpi)

# Expected: median PFS 18 mo (osimertinib) vs 10 mo (erlotinib)
# Hazard ratio = 0.55; alpha = 0.05 (two-sided); power = 0.80
hr     <- 0.55
median1<- 10      # comparator median PFS (months)
median2<- 18      # intervention median PFS
alpha  <- 0.05
power  <- 0.80

n_per_arm <- ssizeCT.default(
  power=power, k=1, pE=0.6, pC=0.8,
  RR=hr, alpha=alpha
)
print(n_per_arm)  # ≈ 110 per arm → total 220
import numpy as np
from scipy.stats import norm

# Sample size for two-arm survival comparison (Schoenfeld 1981)
hr    = 0.55
alpha = 0.05
power = 0.80
z_a   = norm.ppf(1 - alpha/2)
z_b   = norm.ppf(power)

# Expected event rate during follow-up (combined arms)
p_event = 0.70

events_needed = (z_a + z_b)**2 / (np.log(hr))**2 * 4
n_total = events_needed / p_event
print(round(n_total))  # ≈ 220

📝 隨堂測驗

Q1. 下列哪一項不屬於 PICO 框架的四個元件？

A. Population
B. Intervention
C. Cost
D. Outcome
Cost 不在 PICO 中。第四個元件是 Outcome（結果指標）；C 代表 Comparison（對照組）。Cost is not part of PICO. The four elements are Population, Intervention, Comparison, Outcome.

Q2. 對於罕見疾病，最適合的觀察性設計？

A. 前瞻性世代研究Prospective cohort
B. 病例對照 (Case-Control)Case-control
C. 橫斷研究Cross-sectional
D. 生態學研究Ecological study
Case-control 可從少量已知病例反查暴露因子，遠比追蹤大量健康人等病例出現有效率。Case-control studies start from existing cases and look back for exposures — far more efficient than following huge healthy cohorts.

Q3. HARKing 會帶來哪一種主要問題？

A. 樣本量不足Insufficient sample size
B. 第一型錯誤膨脹，研究無法重現Inflated Type I error and irreproducibility
C. 資料隱私洩漏Data privacy leak
D. 違反 GDPRViolation of GDPR
看完結果再「補上」假說會把巧合的關聯當成發現，導致 false positive 暴增，後續研究無法重現。"Fitting" a hypothesis after seeing results turns coincidences into "findings," inflating false positives and ruining reproducibility.

Q4. FINER 中的「N」代表？

A. Necessary（必要的）Necessary
B. Novel（新穎的）Novel
C. Non-invasive（非侵入性）Non-invasive
D. Numeric（數值化）Numeric
FINER = Feasible / Interesting / Novel / Ethical / Relevant。FINER = Feasible / Interesting / Novel / Ethical / Relevant.
