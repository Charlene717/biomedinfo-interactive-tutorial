生物醫學資訊學研究技術

從研究設計到論文發表——14 個主題帶你掌握生醫資訊研究的完整方法論，搭配互動模擬、R/Python 程式碼與單元測驗。

From research design to publication — master the complete methodology of biomedical informatics research in 14 topics, with interactive simulations, R/Python code, and quizzes.

01 核心研究流程

🧭
Step 1

研究設計與假說

以 PICO / FINER 框架建立可檢驗的研究問題；理解觀察性、實驗性與系統性回顧的差異。

Build testable research questions with PICO / FINER frameworks; understand observational, experimental, and systematic review designs.
📚
Step 2

文獻檢索與綜述

運用 PubMed、MeSH、布林邏輯與 PRISMA 流程，建立完整且可重現的文獻檢索策略。

Use PubMed, MeSH, Boolean logic, and the PRISMA workflow to build complete, reproducible literature search strategies.
🗄️
Step 3

公開資料庫探索

熟悉 NCBI、Ensembl、UCSC、GEO、TCGA、UK Biobank 等核心資料庫的存取與限制。

Master access to NCBI, Ensembl, UCSC, GEO, TCGA, UK Biobank and understand their data access policies.
⚖️
Step 4

研究倫理與資料治理

IRB / IACUC 審查、HIPAA / GDPR 合規、知情同意與 FAIR 資料原則。

IRB/IACUC review, HIPAA/GDPR compliance, informed consent, and the FAIR data principles.
📐
Step 5

樣本量與檢定力

理解 α、β、效應量與檢定力之間的權衡，估算研究所需樣本量。

Understand the trade-off between α, β, effect size, and power. Estimate sample size for your study.
📊
Step 6

統計假設檢定

在參數與無母數檢定間做選擇：t-test、Wilcoxon、ANOVA、chi-square 與其前提。

Choose between parametric and non-parametric tests: t-test, Wilcoxon, ANOVA, chi-square — and their assumptions.
🎯
Step 7

多重檢定校正

控制 FWER 與 FDR：Bonferroni、Benjamini-Hochberg、Storey q-value 的選擇邏輯。

Control FWER vs FDR: when to use Bonferroni, Benjamini-Hochberg, or Storey q-values.
⚠️
Step 8

偏差與批次效應

辨識 selection bias、information bias、confounding 與 batch effect，並學會校正策略。

Identify selection bias, information bias, confounding, and batch effects — and how to correct for each.
⏱️
Step 9

存活分析

Kaplan-Meier 曲線、log-rank 檢定、Cox 比例風險模型與 censoring 概念。

Kaplan-Meier curves, log-rank test, Cox proportional hazards model, and the concept of censoring.

Fixed/random effects models, forest plots, heterogeneity (I²), publication bias, GRADE.

Version control (Git), containerization (Docker), workflows (Snakemake/Nextflow), Quarto/Rmd reports.
🎨
Advanced

數據視覺化原則

Chart type selection, colorblind-safe palettes, identifying misleading plots, ggplot2/matplotlib practice.
✍️
Advanced

科學寫作與發表

IMRaD 結構、期刊選擇、同儕審查、preprint 文化與 ORCID、作者貢獻 (CRediT)。

IMRaD structure, journal selection, peer review, preprint culture, ORCID, CRediT author contributions.
